All-but-one of these people are lying (abootpal) is an open-source, online multiplayer adaptation of the Technical Difficulties' game Two Of These People Are Lying. It was developed by Dylan J. White.

Every turn, players get a random article title from Wikipedia, and each one must describe the contents of that article to a judging player. The catch is that only one of the players gets to read the contents of the article, while the others have to make something up based only on the title. The judge must then try to figure out which player is the one telling the truth.

As a player describing an article, you get points if the judge chooses you as the truth-telling player. That means that you want to sound as convincing as you can, whether you saw the article's contents or not. As the judge, you get points if you correctly identify which player was telling the truth.

The source code for abootpal is publicly available on GitHub. This is the place to go if you have ideas for how to improve the game, have found a bug, or would like to contribute something to the game's code.
